## About Ecotone Land & Water

An ecotone is the transitional area where two ecosystems meet. 

We work at the intersection of land and water—helping clients design, restore, and steward landscapes that function as part of larger ecological systems.

## About Jordan

Jordan Gilbert is a fluvial geomorphologist by training with a strong background in ecology. He has a PhD in Geosciences, and has focused his career on restoring riverscapes and landscapes.
