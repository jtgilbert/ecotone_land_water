title: 🌼 Waterwise Landscape Conversion
sidebar_position: 1
--- 

Most lawns in Utah were never designed for this climate—they require constant inputs just to survive.
We take a different approach, designing landscapes that work with the natural systems here—reducing water use, improving soil and drainage, and creating something that thrives naturally and fits the place.
The result is a landscape that uses significantly less water, requires less maintenance over time, and looks more refined and intentional than a traditional lawn.
